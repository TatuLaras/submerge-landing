Immersion? ü§î

Language immersion aka. consuming content that's in a foreign language for language learning purposes is by far the most effective language learning method according to current research. It's the most natural, too. Babies don't open a textbook to learn their native language, they listen to their parents talking and they read their little children's books.

Submersive Tracker is a tracker of your immersion: the time you spend immersing, what kind of immersion you do (reading, listening, active, passive), and the content you choose to do it with. This app gives you an intuitive interface to add that data to the current day or edit previous days. Afterward, you can look at aggregate data graphs and statistics of that data or get a more detailed view of it in a spreadsheet-like form. The app also includes some social features, letting you add friends and see their recent immersion data.


Quick tour üîç

Here is a quick tour of the features Submerge has to offer!

  • A detailed view of immersion data as graphs and statistics
  • An overview of immersion days in a spreadsheet-like form
  • A convenient interface for adding immersion time, equipped with a stopwatch and a timer
  • A convenient interface for adding content, which gives suggestions depending on what you and others have immersed with
  • Social features, which let you befriend people and add them to your dashboard, which lets you see their recent immersion data
  • A leaderboard and a list of the most popular immersion materials can also be found in the social features category
